WishCraft - Personalized Wish Templates

WishCraft is your go-to platform for creating beautifully designed wish templates. Whether you want to celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, or special occasions, WishCraft provides customizable HTML templates, including birthday countdown cards, greeting cards, and more. Simply generate, copy, and share stunning wish templates with your loved ones!

🌟 How to Use WishCraft

1.

Choose a Template 🎨
Chose the available templates and click on your favorite one.

2.

Review and Create Template 🔍
Preview the template and click the "Create Template" button if you like it.

3.

Fill in the Details 📝
Enter the required details:

  • Create Your ID/Code (to access your template later, don't share it!)
  • Birthday Person’s Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Wish or Message
  • Upload Some Pictures
  • Select Background Music and preview it
4.

Generate Your Template 🎉
Click the "Create Template" button to finalize it.

5.

Download Your Template 📥
Click the "Copy" button to Copy url and share your template.

💡 Tip: You can access your template later using the ID/Code you created!
